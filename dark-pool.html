🌑 Dark Pool Tracker

Off-exchange institutional block trades — where the big money moves quietly

What are dark pools? Dark pools are private exchanges where institutions trade large blocks of stock away from public markets — to avoid moving the price. About 35–40% of all US equity volume trades in dark pools. When dark pool volume spikes in a stock, it often signals that institutions are quietly building or exiting positions. High dark pool buy % = bullish signal.
