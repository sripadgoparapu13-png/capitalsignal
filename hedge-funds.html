🏦 Hedge Fund Tracker

What the world's best investors are buying and selling — based on quarterly 13F filings

How this works: Hedge funds managing over $100M must file a 13F form with the SEC every quarter, disclosing their US stock holdings. This data is public — filed 45 days after quarter end. It's a lagging indicator (up to 45 days old), but it shows where the smartest money in the world is positioned. Data shown is from Q4 2024 13F filings.
