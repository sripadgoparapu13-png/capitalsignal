Our Story
Built by a student,
for everyone.

CapitalSignal started as a high school project and became a real financial platform. Here's the story behind it.

S
Sripad
Founder & Developer — Age 16

I'm a 16-year-old high school sophomore who had absolutely zero coding experience when I started building CapitalSignal. I'd always been interested in investing but found every good tool either too expensive or too complicated for someone just starting out.

So I decided to build my own. I taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript from scratch — learning by doing, breaking things, fixing them, and slowly understanding how it all works. What started as a simple prototype became a full financial platform with real-time data, charts, news, a stock screener, portfolio tracker, and more.

CapitalSignal is proof that you don't need a computer science degree, years of experience, or a big team to build something real. You just need curiosity and the willingness to keep going when things break.

🎯 The Mission

Wall Street has always had an information advantage. Professional investors pay thousands of dollars per month for Bloomberg terminals, research reports, and data feeds. Regular people — students, young professionals, everyday investors — are left with cluttered free tools packed with ads and paywalls.

CapitalSignal exists to close that gap. Our mission is to give every investor — regardless of age, experience, or budget — access to the same quality of financial analysis that professionals use. For free. Always.

We believe that better-informed investors make better decisions. And better financial decisions create better lives.
