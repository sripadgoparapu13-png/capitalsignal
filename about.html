My Story

I'm Sripad, a 16-year-old sophomore from Dallas, Texas. I built CapitalSignal because I got interested in investing but couldn't find a free tool that gave me the kind of deep analysis that professional investors use — analyst ratings, insider trading activity, dividend history, signal scores, and real AI insights all in one place.

So I decided to build it myself. With no prior coding experience, I started from zero — learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, figuring out how APIs work, deploying to Vercel, and debugging things that broke in unexpected ways. Every feature on this site is something I built because I personally wanted it.

CapitalSignal started as a simple stock search tool. Then I added charts. Then news. Then analyst ratings. Then a whole market heatmap. It's grown into something I'm genuinely proud of — and I'm just getting started.

🎯 The Mission

Institutional investors have Bloomberg terminals, Wall Street analysts, and proprietary data. Everyone else gets noise. CapitalSignal exists to give the next generation of investors access to the same quality of analysis — completely free.
