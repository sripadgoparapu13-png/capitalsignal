🎯 Unusual Options Activity

Large options trades that signal where smart money is positioning — sweeps, blocks, and golden tickets

What is unusual options activity? When institutions or "smart money" makes large, out-of-the-money options bets — especially sweeps (urgent buys across multiple exchanges) — it often signals that someone knows something. These trades stand out because they're larger than normal relative to open interest. They're not guaranteed to work, but they're watched closely by professional traders.
